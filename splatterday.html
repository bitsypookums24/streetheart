Help paint the town red (and blue...and yellow...) by joining in our community day in sunny Santa Ana. Join resident artists as they teach kids the fundimentals of art through activities for the whole family. Join the community in painting a mural designed by our Artist -in-Residence Rufino Vasquez.  Sidewalk chalk, acrylic paint,  and other fun art projects  are also available.

RUFINO VASQUEZ

is a Southern California native. The son of two Mexican immigrants, he went to Cal Arts to pursue a degree in Studio Art, focusing on mixed media. He has had the opportunity to create works in Los Angeles and San Antonio. He is now a resident teacher at StreetheArt.
